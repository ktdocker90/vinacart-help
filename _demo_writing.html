Adding or editing products

In your store's Admin area, go to the Products section Catalog > Products.

Product Condition
Available - if product's quantity or product's option quantity more than 0. Note: related to your track stock settings see

Status: MUST be "ON" for product to appear in your store.

Featured Product: MUST be "ON" for product to appear as featured in your store. If you use default template featured products appear on Home Page.

Product Name: The product name as it will appear in the front-end.

Description Note

This uses the HTML-Editor so you can add HTML, images and FLASH content.

  • Change Logo.

    Logo related to template settings. In order to edit logo your need to locate storefront template settings in Admin.

    Log in to your store's Admin area and go to the Design > Templates section. Click Edit button on your storefront template. On template settings page click on logo image to change it and a popup will appear allowing you to upload a picture you like from your local computer
